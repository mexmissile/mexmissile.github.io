The guitar

I've been playing the guitar on and off for many years, I finally, seriously, started to practice it since the start of the pandemic. I don't know how to play any songs which is dissapointing but I know how to play some scales and random stuff. I've been learning to improvise and understand the fretboard. I like to practice by playing a random backing track in any key, from youtube, and improvise! Or aleast try 😖 It's what I do when I procrastinate doing school work.

Skateboarding

In order to become cooler, wear my hat backwards and shop at pacsun, I picked up skateboarding. I started July of 2020 and my goal is to learn the kickflip by July of 2021. I hardly have any free time to ride around, but when I do it's ultra fun. It's calming and therapeutic to plug in your headphones and ride around while listening to the sound of the wheels in the background. I also hope to learn and master riding the pools in the skateparks.
